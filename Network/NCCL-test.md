### Busbw vs 실제 대역폭
```
Busbw = Algbw × (2 × (N-1) / N)

125 노드:
Busbw = Algbw × (2 × 124 / 125)
Busbw = Algbw × 1.984


Busbw = 555 GB/s (목표)
Algbw = 555 / 1.984 = 279.74 GB/s
```
